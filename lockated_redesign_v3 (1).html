Property Lifecycle

Five stages.
One platform.

No more switching tools mid-project. Lockated follows the property from first enquiry all the way through to community management.

Stage 01
Lead Management
Capture and track leads from every channel. Brokers, portals, referrals, walk-ins. Full pipeline visibility with conversion analytics.
Stage 02
Site Management
Real-time construction progress, task assignments and material tracking. Every block, every floor, updated as it happens.
Stage 03
Snagging & QC
Digital defect logging with photo evidence. Assign, track and resolve snags before possession. Compliance-ready audit trails.
Stage 04
Handover
Scheduled unit handovers with digital checklists, owner documentation and sign-off. Zero paper, zero confusion.
Stage 05
Community Management
Society billing, visitor control, helpdesk and resident engagement all in one app. The relationship continues post-possession.
